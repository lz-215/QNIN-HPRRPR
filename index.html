Five Nights at Freddy's

Survive five nights as a security guard in a haunted pizzeria with murderous animatronics

4.8

Five Nights at Freddy's is a terrifying point-and-click survival horror game where you work as a night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Your job seems simple: monitor the security cameras from midnight to 6 AM. However, there's a deadly secret - the restaurant's animatronic mascots come to life at night and have a murderous streak. With limited power to control the doors, lights, and security cameras, you must survive five nights while managing your resources carefully to keep the animatronics from reaching your office.

Survival Tips

  • Conserve power - only use lights and doors when necessary
  • Check Pirate Cove frequently to monitor Foxy
  • Listen for audio cues to know when animatronics are near
  • Develop a camera-checking pattern to track all animatronics
  • If you hear footsteps, be ready to close the appropriate door

Animatronic Behaviors

  • Freddy: Moves directly to your office, check right door light
  • Bonnie: Approaches from the left, check left door light
  • Chica: Approaches from the right, listen for kitchen sounds
  • Foxy: Runs from Pirate Cove if not watched regularly
